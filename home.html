About Virat Kohli

Born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi, Virat Kohli quickly rose through the ranks to become one of the world's best cricketers. He is known for his aggressive playstyle, phenomenal batting skills, and inspiring leadership as India's captain.

Virat's career started at a young age, and he captained India to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup. Since then, he has broken multiple cricket records and remains a role model for young aspiring athletes.
